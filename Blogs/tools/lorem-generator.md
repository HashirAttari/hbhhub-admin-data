## Lorem Ipsum Generator: Quickly Create Placeholder Text

Creating placeholder text for designs, websites, or documents is simple with the **Lorem Ipsum Generator**. This tool lets you generate realistic-looking text tailored to your needs, with control over paragraphs, sentences, and words.

---

### How to Use the Lorem Ipsum Generator

1. **Set Paragraphs**
   Use the slider to choose how many paragraphs you want to generate.

2. **Adjust Sentences per Paragraph**
   The range slider lets you define a minimum and maximum number of sentences per paragraph.

3. **Adjust Words per Sentence**
   Another range slider allows you to control the number of words in each sentence.

4. **Start with "Lorem Ipsum"**
   Toggle the checkbox if you want the first paragraph to begin with the classic **"Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet."**

5. **Generate or Refresh Text**
   Click the **Refresh** button to generate new content based on your settings.

6. **Copy Your Text**
   Click the **Copy** button to copy the generated paragraphs to your clipboard instantly.
