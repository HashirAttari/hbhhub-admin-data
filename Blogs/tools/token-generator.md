## Token Generator: Create Secure Tokens & Passwords Instantly

Generating strong, secure tokens or passwords can be tricky without the right tool. The **Token Generator** lets you create random strings tailored to your needs, whether for passwords, API keys, or authentication tokens.

---

### How to Use the Token Generator

1. **Choose Character Types**
   Toggle which characters to include: **Uppercase, Lowercase, Numbers, or Symbols**. You can combine any combination depending on your security needs.

2. **Set the Length**
   Use the slider to choose the desired length of your token, from 4 to 512 characters.

3. **Generate Your Token**
   The token updates automatically based on your settings, or click the **refresh button** to generate a new one instantly.

4. **Copy the Token**
   Click the output box to copy your token for immediate use.
