## Percentage (Marks) Calculator – Complete Guide

The Percentage Calculator is a simple yet essential tool designed to help students quickly calculate their exam or test percentages. Instead of manually applying formulas, this tool gives you instant and accurate results with minimal effort.

Whether you're checking your school results, college grades, or test scores, this calculator makes the process fast and error-free.

---

### How to Use

Using this calculator is easy and takes only a few seconds:

1. **Enter Total Marks**
   Input the total marks of your exam or subject (e.g., 500, 1000).

2. **Enter Obtained Marks**
   Enter the marks you scored.

3. **Click on “Calculate”**
   The calculator will instantly display your percentage.

4. **Reset if Needed**
   Use the reset button to clear inputs and calculate again.

---

### Percentage Formula Explained

The calculator uses a simple mathematical formula:

**Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100**

Example:
If you scored 450 out of 500:
(450 ÷ 500) × 100 = **90%**

This tool automates this calculation and reduces the chance of mistakes.
